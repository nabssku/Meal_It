Frequently Asked Questions

View all
How does the AI optimize my budget? expand_more
Our proprietary AI analyzes local vendor pricing, seasonal ingredient availability, and your specific dietary needs. It cross-references current discounts across all verified vendors to suggest ingredient swaps that lower costs without compromising on your nutritional goals.
Are all vendors really verified? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. We have a 4-step verification process that includes food safety certifications, ethical sourcing audits, and pricing transparency checks. Every vendor on MEALIT is a partner we trust for quality and value.
Can I sync with my fitness tracker? expand_more
Mealit currently supports direct syncing with Apple Health, Google Fit, and Fitbit. This allows us to adjust your meal recommendations based on your actual caloric expenditure and macronutrient requirements for the day.
How do I install the PWA? expand_more
On iOS, tap the 'Share' icon in Safari and select 'Add to Home Screen'. On Android/Chrome, tap the three dots in the corner and select 'Install App'. This gives you offline access and faster loading times.
