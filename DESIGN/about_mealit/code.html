Our Story

MEALIT was born in a cramped studio apartment where a student and a busy tech professional realized they were spending too much on takeout and losing track of their health goals.

The challenge wasn't just finding recipes—it was finding the *right* recipes that fit a strict budget without compromising on the nutrients needed for a demanding lifestyle. We built MEALIT to automate the stress of "What's for dinner?" while keeping your wallet full.
